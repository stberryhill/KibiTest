# Should you use KibiTest?
## Knowable
KibiTest is for small projects that don't need a bloated, complicated testing framework. You can literally learn the entire framework by reading the only source file in about 30 seconds.
## Portable & Embeddable
Being a single header with a size of just 1022 bytes makes KibiTest a good option for embedded projects with harsh memory limitations. Being written in Ansi C takes it's portabbility and embeddability to a whole new level.
## Easy to Modify
Because it's so small, tinkering with it to suit your needs is a piece of cake.
## Philosophy of Tiny Projects
The philosophy behind KibiTest isn't just to be super minimal, but that if your tests start getting really complicated, your project may be getting to big and could be split into multiple smaller ones. In this way you build up your own ecosystem of single-purpose, easily maintainable tools.
